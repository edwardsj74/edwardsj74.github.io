1986: The Beginning

The first Castlevania releases on the NES, introducing players to Simon Belmont and the cursed castle of Dracula.

1997: Symphony of the Night

A turning point. Players control Alucard in a non-linear adventure, pioneering the “Metroidvania” genre.

2010: Lords of Shadow

A reimagined tale that revived the franchise for modern consoles, blending myth and tragedy in cinematic style.

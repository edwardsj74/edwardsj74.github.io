1986: The Beginning

The original Castlevania debuts on the NES, introducing Simon Belmont and Dracula’s cursed castle.

1997: Symphony of the Night

Alucard’s journey redefined exploration and combat, creating the “Metroidvania” genre that still thrives today.

2010: Lords of Shadow

A cinematic reimagining, blending myth and tragedy to reinvent the eternal war between light and darkness.

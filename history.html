1986: The Beginning

The original Castlevania debuts on the NES, introducing Simon Belmont and Dracula’s haunted castle.

1997: Symphony of the Night

A revolutionary entry starring Alucard, blending exploration and combat to define the “Metroidvania” genre.

2010: Lords of Shadow

A cinematic retelling reimagines Dracula’s origin, blending mythology and tragedy for a new era of fans.

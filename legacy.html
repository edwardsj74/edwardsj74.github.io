Why Castlevania Still Matters

Castlevania is more than just a game franchise, it’s a gothic myth reborn in every era. Its influence can be seen in countless games, films, and anime. From its pixelated origins to Netflix’s acclaimed adaptation, the series’ dark romanticism continues to enchant fans.

The Gothic Legacy

The series inspired the “Metroidvania” genre, influencing games like Hollow Knight, Bloodstained, and Dark Souls. Its gothic tone (stained glass, candlelight, ancient ruins) inspired a generation of creators who found beauty in darkness.
